Habit Tracking

Track every habit, your way.

From daily streaks to sobriety counters — Forge handles every type of habit without compromise.

Streaks
Day streaks, per habit streaks, perfect day streaks. Every dimension of consistency tracked.
Timed Habits
Read 20 minutes, meditate 10 minutes. Full screen countdown keeps you present and honest.
Multi-completion
Some habits need doing twice. Morning and night — Forge tracks each completion separately.
Rest Days
Life happens. Earn Rest Days to protect your streak without guilt or punishment.
Counter Habits
Quit alcohol, quit smoking. Your counter counts up — days, hours, minutes sober.
Flexible Scheduling
Daily, specific days, morning or evening. Your routine runs on your schedule, not the app's defaults.

Compete

Habits hit different when there's something at stake.

Forge turns consistency into competition — climb leagues, battle friends, take on bots at any difficulty.

01
League System
Bronze to Ruby. Earn XP by completing habits, hit perfect days, maintain streaks. Promote weekly or risk relegation — real stakes every Monday.
02
Friend Battles
Challenge a friend on any habit. 7 days, a month — whoever shows up more consistently wins. Finish a battle and choose to keep the habit permanently.
03
Bot Battles
No friends available? Take on AI bots across Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced difficulty. Free users get 3 battles per month — Pro gets unlimited.
